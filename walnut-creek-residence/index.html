Walnut Creek Residence

Sole Designer
2011-ongoing

How can a porch open up a house?

The design for an outdoor living room and porch creates visual and physical connections between the existing residence and its backyard, while providing a shield from the sun. The sliding planes of the roofs compliment the horizontality of the ranch house and create distinct zones of habitation.

As sole designer, responsibilities included in-depth design sessions with the clients and all aspects of design, from concept to material selection.
