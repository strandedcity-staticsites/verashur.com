Incubator Space

VITAL environments, Inc.
Role: Lead Designer & Project Manager
2014

How can you make a space that fosters an innovation community?

Located in downtown Honolulu, this co-working space for a clean energy accelerator and its portfolio companies seeks to provide a flexible work environment and showcase the breadth and reach of its community. Through deep engagement with the client and stakeholders, we crafted a space that reflected the organization’s mission and values, and made a home for its team.

As project lead, responsibilities included the design of the space and custom furniture elements, choosing furnishings and finishes, UX design, documentation, working closely with the client and contractors, and construction administration.
