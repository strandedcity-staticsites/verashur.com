+ Shelf

Artist-in-Residency at Autodesk
2015

A prototype for an easy-to-assemble shelving system that can be customized to suit one’s space and contents. The 3d printed +’s hold together each individual cubby. Gaps between each cubby, equal to the thickness of each +, give the shelves the feeling of floating in space.
